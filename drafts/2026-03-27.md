## 5. Thread hook + outline (Perplexity Personal Computer and always-on AI)

**Hook:**

Perplexity just announced a Mac mini that runs AI 24/7 as your digital proxy. monitors triggers, executes tasks, merges local files with cloud. the shift from "AI you open" to "AI that's always running" is the actual product change happening right now.

<!-- 255 chars -->

**Thread outline:**

1. current AI is pull-based: you open ChatGPT, type a question, close the tab
2. Perplexity Personal Computer is push-based: AI monitors your filesystem, calendar, triggers — acts without being asked
3. same pattern shift that happened with email (pull) to Slack notifications (push) a decade ago
4. for devs: CI/CD was this for code deployment. now it's happening for knowledge work and daily workflows
5. for apps like Clio: background location monitoring + ambient narration without opening the app — always-on context
6. the tradeoff: always-on AI means always-on data access. privacy model needs to be local-first or it breaks trust
7. prediction: in 12 months every serious AI product ships a background daemon, not just a chat window
